Biography

Malcolm L. Branch is an aerospace engineer and independent researcher located in north Texas, United States. Malcolm received B.S. and M.S. degrees in aerospace engineering from the University of Texas at Arlington in 2016—with a minor in mechanical engineering—and 2021, respectively. They are a member of AIAA and ASME professional organizations. Their research interests lie in propulsion systems, detonation-based combustion, and aeroacoustics. 

Malcolm Branch's work has been conducted on the historical and unceded lands of the Jumanos, Kiikaapoi (Kickapoo), Nʉmʉnʉʉ Sookobitʉ (Comanche), Wichita, and Tawakoni peoples. You can learn more about these native inhabitants of North America, those where you live, and others at native-land.ca.
